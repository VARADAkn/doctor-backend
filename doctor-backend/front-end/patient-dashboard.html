Welcome to SkinScan AI

Our Mission

SkinScan AI is a state-of-the-art dermatological screening platform designed to assist in the early detection of skin cancer.

Core Capabilities

  • 1. Instant AI Analysis: Analyze lesions in real-time.
  • 2. Large-Scale Screening: Trained on 30,000+ clinical images.
  • 3. Secure Platform: Your data and privacy are our top priority.

Furthermore: Understanding Melanoma

The Severity of Melanoma

Melanoma represents only about 1% of skin cancers but causes a large majority of skin cancer deaths.

Risk Factors to Consider

  • UV Exposure: Sunburns and tanning beds significantly increase risk.
  • Mole Count: Having many moles or unusual moles.

How to Spot Potential Risks (ABCDE Rule)

  • Asymmetry
  • Border
  • Color
  • Diameter
  • Evolving
